About Prayagraj

Prayagraj, formerly known as Allahabad, is a city with rich history and religious significance. It is famous for the confluence of the three rivers – Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati, known as the Triveni Sangam. The city is a center of cultural and religious events, especially the Maha Kumbh Mela, which attracts millions of devotees from all over the world.

The city also boasts various historical and cultural landmarks, making it a significant destination for tourists and pilgrims alike. The sacred ghats, majestic forts, and historical temples contribute to its spiritual charm and allure.

Famous Places to Visit

Triveni Sangam

The sacred confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers, revered for its spiritual significance.

Allahabad Fort

A historic fort built by Emperor Akbar, offering panoramic views of the confluence of Ganga and Yamuna.

Anand Bhavan

The residence of the Nehru family, now a museum showcasing India's freedom struggle.
