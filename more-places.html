More Places to Visit in Prayagraj

Khusro Bagh

A historical garden and tomb complex, known for its Mughal architecture and serene environment.

Allahabad Museum

Home to an extensive collection of artifacts, sculptures, and historical relics showcasing the region's rich heritage.

Chandrashekhar Azad Park

A historical park named after the freedom fighter Chandrashekhar Azad, known for its lush greenery and monument.

New Yamuna Bridge

A modern bridge spanning the Yamuna River, offering panoramic views of the city and river.

Hanuman Mandir

A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, known for its religious significance and vibrant atmosphere.

All Saints Cathedral

An architectural marvel with stunning Gothic design, offering a glimpse into the colonial past of Prayagraj.
